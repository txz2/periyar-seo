What Curry Goes Best with Appam in Kerala?

Periyar Thattukada · Kerala Food Pairings

Appam is one of the most versatile Kerala breads. The thin, crispy edges and soft, pillowy centre are designed to be paired with something saucy. But which curry goes best with appam? In Kerala, there is no single answer. The pairing depends on the time of day, region, and tradition. Here are the most beloved combinations.

Appam with Vegetable Stew

This is the most classic Kerala pairing. The stew is mild, coconut-milk based, and loaded with vegetables like potato, carrot, and green peas. The gentle sweetness of the coconut milk and the softness of the vegetables contrast beautifully with the lacy edges of the appam. This combination is found in Christian Kerala homes and is the traditional Sunday breakfast in many families across Kochi and Ernakulam.

Appam with Kadala Curry

For those who prefer a bolder pairing, kadala curry, the spiced black chickpea curry, is the answer. The thick, intensely flavoured gravy soaks into the soft centre of the appam and the contrast of textures is satisfying in a completely different way from the stew. This is the nadan thattukada version of appam, the one you find at street-side eateries rather than hotels.

Appam with Chicken Curry

Kerala chicken curry with coconut milk is another popular appam pairing. The medium-spiced gravy with whole spices, curry leaves, and coconut milk is rich but not overpowering, making it ideal for appam. This combination is common at weddings and special occasions across Kerala.

Appam with Egg Curry

A simple but deeply satisfying pairing. Hard-boiled eggs in a coconut-based curry with shallots, mustard, and curry leaves, ladled over appam, is fast-food Kerala at its finest. It is one of the most common thattukada breakfast combinations across the state.

Which Is the Best Pairing?

If you are eating appam for breakfast, go with stew or egg curry. If you are eating in the evening at a thattukada, kadala curry or a simple coconut milk chutney is the traditional pairing. Chicken curry is the celebration option. All four are correct depending on context.
